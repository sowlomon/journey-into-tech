WELCOME TO MY BLOG!

My name is Solomon Adache, i am 22 years old, i am a Nigerian and a student of kaduna polytechnic studying computer engineering at the HND level. i want to become a software engineer after graduating so i am working really hard to get the necessary skills i require to become a good developer.I am going to talk a little about how i fell in love with technology.


HOW I FELL IN LOVE WITH TECH

It all started when i was in primary 4,i fell in love with the computer system though i never had one at that time but all thanks to my dad who is a computer scientist, he introduced me to computers, through him i saw some cool things i could do with the computer system including playinggames of course, lol ,my dad always took i and my siblings to his office and gave us access to his computer system through which i learnt about microsoft word, i also learnt how to draw using paint tool and so on,he took tutorials for some students on microsoft word, so most times i do assist him with the students which made his work easier.

After learning much about computers through my dad it helped me so much because i offered computer science while in primary school then and at the end of my study in primary school, i came out the best in computer science at this point i knew that this was for me, but i wanted to become an architect when i grew up at that time but i still had so much love for the computer.

To cut the long story short after graduating from secondary school in 2016 i applied for architecture in ABU(Ahmadu bello university) but i did not get the admission all thanks to God that i already applied to study computer engineering in kaduna polytechnic,i was given the admission and here i am in HND now i guess it was all God's plan because i have no regrets at all. It was during my ND1 in school that i first learnt about programming then we were being taught FOTRAN programming language i never understood that language as at the time but i managed to pass the course, after my ND1 we were sent out for industrial experience(SIWES) it was then every thing changed i found my love "coding".There i was sent to the computer repairs department because i studied computer engineering but they also had a computer programming department that taught programming,i decided to visit the department to see what they were doing,there i met a young boy, i cant really remember his name though, i saw him scrolling through a web page(a very beautiful web page) then i picked interest and asked him what web page was that, he looked at me and said its the web page he created,i was amazed, he showed me how he created the web page with the codes he wrote it was then i fell in love with programming. I picked interest because i am a very creative person and i love creative processes,though i never really went deep into programming at that time because i never had a system,so after i graduated from ND(national diploma) i went on for my 1 year I.T(industrial training) during this period i worked with nigerian breweries(NB) after my 1 year I.T my dad got me a computer system that was towards the end of 2019,and by 2020 i was to resume back to continue my schooling(HND) which i did, this was the year the corana virus struck and i left school to come back home. While i was home i promised my self that i was going to learn more about programming, i made a lot of research and found out that python was the best language to start with as a beginner so i downloaded lots of python tutorials from scratch and i started learning the fundamentals of programming after about 5 months of learning i was able to work on three projects with the help of tutorial videos.Some of the projects i worked on are listed below:
